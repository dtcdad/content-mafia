The Problem

Local businesses and creators face real challenges that no one is solving.

For Brands

  • Need consistent content for social media and ads
  • Don't know how to work directly with local creators
  • Agencies and influencers are expensive and complicated
  • Need to own their content strategy and budget
  • No easy way to find local creators outside of major US markets

For Creators

  • Individuals want opportunities to create content and get paid
  • Most creators are not business-savvy

  • People think only influencers can be creators
  • People create engaging content every day but don't monetize it
